Professional Summary

I am Tahani, an engineer, educator, and instructional designer with over 7 years of experience in education, training, research, and learning experience design. I am skilled in creating Web VR, 3D scenes, 360° interactive environments, and immersive experiences including vibe coding, modelling, sculpting, texturing, lighting, animation, and video editing.

I am also proficient in storytelling, scriptwriting, storyboarding, content creation, and video production planning and management. My work combines strong educational foundations with creative and cinematic approaches to make learning more engaging, interactive, and memorable.

I have experience leveraging AI-powered creative tools to generate and enhance multimedia assets, including images, audio, 3D objects, HDRI environments, and other digital media, supporting efficient and innovative content production.

I completed “The Art of Storytelling” course by Pixar Animation Studios, which strengthened my skills in narrative structure, visual storytelling, and cinematic techniques for engaging educational and creative content.

Furthermore, I create content about 3D and CGI animated movies for entertainment and educational purposes.
